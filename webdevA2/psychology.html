Psychology

As mentioned before, the human mind subconsciously associates and draws conclusions from colour.


Red

Red symbolises a number of things, both good and bad. It can represent romance, passion, danger and blood.

Pink

Pink is usually associated with femininity and love, though it can also represent sweetness and health.

Purple

Purple tends to symbolise royalty and nobility. This was due to how difficult it was to attain purple dye back then, making it only a luxury for the rich and noble. It can also represent mystery and magic.

Blue

Blue tends to represent the ocean and sky, which is associated with freedom, imagination and tranquility. It can also represent intelligence, wisdom and sadness.

Green

Green represents growth, safety and luck. It is associated with life and renewal as it is commonly seen in nature. Envy and wealth are also other common associations for the colour green.

Yellow

Yellow is the colour of happiness, youth, sunshine and warmth. It is sometimes also used to represent caution such as in warning signs.

Black

Black is commonly associated with death, mourning, and despair. It usually represents evil in contrast to the colour white. Though it has a lot of negative connotations, it can also represent power, mystery and night.

Grey

Grey usually means neutrality and boredom, due to its distinct lack of colour. It is also associated with aging and hence, wisdom.

White

White symbolises light, purity and innocence, its also commonly used to represent goodness in opposition to the colour black.
